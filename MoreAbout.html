More about us, in case you care.

Is there anything that should go here? Like, how to use our stories, who to contact, etc?

The report, produced by students at the University of Montana School of Journalism, works to put a face on the new meth user base and explain the effects of an uptick in amphetamine use across the state.  The staff is made up of audio, visual and web-focused students in a jointly convened class working to make audio stories consumable on social media. It is an experimental capstone class led by professors Jule and Lee Banville with support from the University of Montana Journalism School. With national news organization partnerships, the feature is set to come out in May 2017. Follow @mtmetheffect on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.

The Meth Effect is a project of the University of Montana Journalism School. Reach the Meth Effect by email at metheffect@gmail.com
